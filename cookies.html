What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and provide information to the owners of the site.

How We Use Cookies

BeatForge AI uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your language preference
  • To remember your audio settings (volume, playback preferences)
  • To understand how you use our website
  • To improve our service based on user behavior

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and choices

These cookies enable the website to remember information that changes the way the website behaves or looks, like your preferred language or the region you are in.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our site

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website.

Functional Cookies

Enable enhanced features

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality such as audio player preferences, volume settings, and playback history.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics and deliver content:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance tracking
  • YouTube: When you play embedded videos from YouTube
  • Cloudflare: For security and performance optimization

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from all or specific websites
  • Set your browser to notify you when cookies are being set

Please note that restricting cookies may impact your experience on our website.
